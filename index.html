About T.H.I.U

TLAfrica and friends are raising funds to build the Thousand Hills International University(THIU) in Rwanda. This app is a major part of that effort, because it enables you to donate directly to this effort via your phone using your credit card and paypal.

Click on the yellow contribute to a hill button below to be taken to a list of hills you can choose to donate to. Click on each hill to get more information about that hill
